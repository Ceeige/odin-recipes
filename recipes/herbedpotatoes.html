Herbed Gold Potatoes

Description

Decadent, soft, gold potatoes recipe with an earthy and rich flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 pounds baby gold potatoes
  • 1 tbsp better, melted
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Garnish: more whole oregano leaves

Steps

      Wash potatoes
      Place potatoes in a large saucepan. Cover with water to 2 inches above potatoes; bring to a boil
      Cover and cook 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender; drain.
