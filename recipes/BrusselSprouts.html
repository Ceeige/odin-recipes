Easy Roasted Brussel Sprouts

Description

Roasted Brussel sprouts are an easy, tasty, and versatile side dish to compliment any of your fall or winter meals!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Brussel sprouts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

      Preheat oven to 400F. Wash brussels and trim off any dry ends. Cut each brussel sprout in half.
      Place the cut brussel on a baking sheet. Drizzle in oil and sprinkle with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
      Roast brussels for 25 minutes in the oven.
      Taste test one brussel sprout and add more seasonings to taste. Serve hot!
